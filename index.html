    Help

    Keep the ball off of the floor of the game. You lose a life every time you run out of balls

    Hit the blocks for points. Yellow = 1 point, Orange = 2 points, Bule = 3 points, Green = 5 points.

    You earn 25 bonus points for every line you clear. You earn a bonus ball every 100 points gained, and your speed will increase after you remove 4 bricks, 12 bricks, 36 bricks, and 62 bricks respectively.

    You can rebind the controls in the settings menu
